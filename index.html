How to play Simon

  1. At the start of the game, Simon will give the first signal by lighting up one colour and playing a sound. Repeat the signal by clicking on or pressing the colour.
  2. Simon will duplicate the first signal and add a second one. Repeat this signal by clicking on or pressing the colours in order.
  3. Simon will duplicate these signals and add one.
  4. Continue playing for as long as you can remember the sequence.
  5. When you make a mistake, the game ends.
